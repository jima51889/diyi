CleanScan Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 8, 2026

CleanScan is designed to scan documents on your device and keep the first version simple.

Data Collection

CleanScan does not collect personal data in the current release.

Documents and Scans

Scanned documents, page images, recognized text, receipts, and generated PDF files are stored locally on your device. CleanScan does not upload scanned documents to a server.

Camera Access

CleanScan requests camera access so you can scan paper documents and receipts into PDFs.

Purchases

The current release is free to use. If paid features are added later, purchases will be handled by Apple through the App Store.

Analytics and Tracking

The current release does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics, or tracking.
