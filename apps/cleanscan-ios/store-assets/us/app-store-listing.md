## Description

CleanScan is a simple PDF scanner for everyday paperwork.

Use it to scan contracts, receipts, notes, forms, IDs, and other paper documents into clean PDF files. After scanning, you can review pages, remove unwanted pages, reorder them, save the document, preview the PDF, and share it through the iOS share sheet.

CleanScan also includes practical document tools:

- Scan multiple pages into one PDF
- Review pages before saving
- Delete and reorder scanned pages
- Recognize text from scanned pages
- Add a handwritten signature
- Create a compressed PDF copy
- Preview and share PDFs
- Keep documents stored on your device

CleanScan is built for students, freelancers, and anyone who needs a scanner that stays out of the way.

No ads. No watermark. No account required.

## Review Notes

CleanScan uses the camera through Apple's document scanner interface to scan paper documents into PDFs.

The current build stores scanned documents locally on the device. It does not require an account and does not upload scanned files to a server.

If testing in Simulator, document scanning may not be available. Please test camera scanning on a physical iPhone.
