# CleanScan US ASO Notes

## Primary Positioning

Private PDF and receipt scanner for students and freelancers.

## Main Search Phrases

- pdf scanner
- document scanner
- scan to pdf
- ocr scanner
- receipt scanner
- pdf signature
- compress pdf
- invoice scanner
- contract scanner

## First Version Positioning

Lead with simple document scanning, not advanced AI or business automation.

Best angle:

CleanScan helps people scan, review, sign, compress, and share PDFs without ads, watermarks, or accounts.

Receipt Mode gives the app a clearer freelancer use case without making claims about tax filing or accounting compliance.

## Avoid

- Overusing "AI"
- Claiming government, legal, or tax compliance
- Claiming guaranteed OCR accuracy
- Claiming secure cloud storage
- Comparing directly with Adobe, CamScanner, or Scanner Pro
- Claiming tax compliance

## Launch Countries

Start with English-speaking markets:

- United States
- Canada
- United Kingdom
- Australia

Keep the first metadata version in English only.
