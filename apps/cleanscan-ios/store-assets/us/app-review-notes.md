# App Review Notes

CleanScan is a free document and receipt scanner.

The app uses Apple's document scanner interface to scan paper documents and receipts into local PDF files. Users can review pages before saving, delete or reorder pages, preview PDFs, share PDFs, run on-device text recognition, create a compressed PDF copy, add a handwritten signature, and export basic receipt records as CSV.

The app does not require an account. The current build does not include ads, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking. Scanned documents are stored locally on the device and are not uploaded to a server.

Please test scanning on a physical iPhone. Document scanning may not be available in the iOS Simulator.

No subscription or in-app purchase is required in this build.
