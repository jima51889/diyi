## Full Description

CleanScan is a clean and privacy-first PDF scanner for everyday documents.

Use it to scan contracts, receipts, lecture notes, tax records, IDs, forms, and paper files into shareable PDFs. CleanScan keeps the first experience simple: open the app, scan pages, preview the PDF, rename the document, and share it through the standard iOS share sheet.

Why CleanScan:

- Fast document scanning
- Multi-page PDF export
- Local document storage
- PDF preview before sharing
- Simple rename and delete actions
- No ads
- No watermark
- Private by default

CleanScan is built for students, freelancers, and small business owners who need a scanner that gets out of the way.
