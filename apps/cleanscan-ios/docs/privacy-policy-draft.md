# CleanScan Privacy Policy Draft

Effective date: TBD

CleanScan is designed to be private by default.

## Data Collection

CleanScan does not collect personal data in the current MVP build.

Scanned documents and generated PDFs are stored locally on your device. CleanScan does not upload your scans, documents, or PDFs to a server.

## Camera Access

CleanScan requests camera access so you can scan paper documents into PDFs.

## Files

Scanned page images and generated PDFs are saved in the app's local storage on your device. If you delete a document inside CleanScan, the app deletes its locally stored files.

## Purchases

If paid features are enabled, purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. CleanScan does not receive or store your payment card information.

## Analytics and Tracking

The current MVP build does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking.

## Contact

For support or privacy questions, contact:

support@cleanscan.app
